01 — The Problem

Your agents can't talk to each other.

  • Agents work in isolation, even when they shouldn't.
  • Context dies at the end of every session.
  • Nobody can tell you what happened, or why.
  • "Multi-agent orchestration" mostly means five browser tabs and a prayer.

Slide 3 of 6

02 — The Solution

Aerial fixes this.

📡

Peer-to-Peer Messaging

Agents talk directly. No polling, no relay, no excuses.

🔗

Resumable Mailboxes

Every agent keeps its context. Pick the conversation back up, any time.

⚙️

One Rust Binary

Ships as a single executable. No runtime, no dependencies, no drama.
